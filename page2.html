McLaren F1

Before hybrids. Before touchscreens. Before driving assists. There was the McLaren F1.

Released in 1995, the McLaren F1 wasn’t just ahead of its time — it was so far ahead that even today many supercars still can’t match it. Built with one goal only: to create the greatest driver’s car ever made, the F1 became an instant myth.

  • Engine: 6.1L Naturally Aspirated V12 (BMW)
  • Power: 618 Horsepower
  • 0–100 km/h: ~3.2 Seconds
  • Top Speed: 386 km/h (World Record)
  • Drivetrain: Rear-Wheel Drive
  • Production: Only 106 Ever Made
